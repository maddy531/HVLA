What we're about


Huron Valley Law Associates, P.C., works with individuals, their families, and small businesses throughout Ann Arbor and Washtenaw County, Livingston County, Monroe County, and Wayne County. We help our clients prepare for the future and offer counseling and assistance during times of conflict. Our mission is to provide you with efficient, personalized, and effective legal representation. Our goal is to help you find the solution that best fits your circumstances, resources, and desires.



Our approach to your case is individualized.


First, we conduct a FREE INITIAL CONSULTATION by phone or in person to determine the source of your problem.

Then, before accepting your case, we research, investigate, and evaluate your options, because sometimes, you don’t need legal counsel to accomplish your goals. If we believe you will benefit from our counsel, however, we work closely with you to assure the best outcome for your situation.

In adversarial situations, we encourage a collaborative approach to problem solving. We stress mediation and negotiation whenever possible, but we provide tough, level-headed representation when cases are contested and litigation is required. We are comfortable inside a courtroom.

We are experienced and compassionate attorneys who recognize that every case is unique. We won’t just tell you what you want to hear, even when the reality of your situation is not pleasant. We will always tell you the truth, and work toward a holistic solution that is consistent with your values and beliefs while protecting your interests.
